About

This site was created in an effort to make the information provided by RIT's Career Services easier to visualize. A limited dataset of only Golisano Co-op information was given to us as a test but we hope to encompass more information from other colleges in the future.

References

We used the D3.js library for help with the visualization and we used Materialize for styling. The logo was made with Logomakr.
